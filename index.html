Hello, I'm so glad
you're here

Silly Stitches started in a tiny flat, with a single crochet hook, a ball of dusty-pink yarn, and a lot of patience. What began as a way to slow down turned into a quiet passion — and eventually, a little shop.

Every piece I make is worked by hand, stitch by stitch. I source soft, natural fibres and take my time with every project. No rush. No mass production. Just honest, cozy craft.

Thank you for supporting handmade. It truly means the world. 🌿

— Emma x
